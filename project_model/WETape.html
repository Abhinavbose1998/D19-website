WETape

A nanotechnology kinesiology tape used for cooling and muscle pain relief for the purpose of sports. It is a physical product that is applied to the skin the same way kinesiology tape is applied. The nanotechnology replicates the exoskeleton of the Namib beetle, which is able to generate water from the air.
