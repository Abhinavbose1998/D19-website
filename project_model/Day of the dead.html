Day of the dead

This project aims to help educate people about the Mexican Festival known as the Day of the Dead through a VR experience. The user learns about this festival by completing objectives provided throughout the game. They will be accompanied by a virtual guide that provides information about the festival as the user completes the various objectives. Interaction is permissible through the virtual reality headset provided. A button on the headset is used to interact with buttons and other elements contained within the game. When the user has the headset on, they can hold down the button to move in the direction they are facing. To turn, they simply move their head and hold the button down to proceed in that direction.
